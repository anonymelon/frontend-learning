The Glory of Cats

Cats are wonderful companions. Whether it's a bottle cap, long string, or your legs, they always find something to chase around.

In fact, cats are so great they even have their own musical . It was inspired by T.S. Eliot's Old Possum's Book of Practical Cats .

Cookie and Woody

Generally considered the sweetest and yet most independent cats in the Pioneer Valley , Cookie and Woody are consistently underestimated by their humble humans.
